Curatorial Design* | Museology & Curation

Getting my hands dirty with curation to fulfil a childhood dream of working at museums. The project focuses on identifying themes and issues within the shifting concept of the process of curation. With the insights gathered curatorial case studies (through participatory experiments) are to be conducted and eventually presented as an exhibition.

Typing on TV | Human-Computer Interaction

The most fun I've ever had working in a team of three. Surveyed users who had problems while typing on television using a remote. Identified insights and opportunity areas for intervention to make the experience better. Ideated, conceptualised and created wireframes for a centralised predictive keyboard to improve the experience of navigating through the letters while typing. Preparing for conference publication.

Check this project!

Digital 3D Crossword | Interaction Design

Starting off from a dad joke, the project compared and evaluated various online crosswords and Erik Westbrook’s 3D crossword. Trends within the category of "Word" games were analysed. Requirements and constraints identified for a possible digital 3D crossword which was eventually conceptualised.

Check this project!

Visual tool for learning about Devanagari script | Curation & Interaction

Exploring my obsession at the time with typography, a short curriculum about the Devanagari script was initially researched and structured. To provide access to this curriculum, ideated and conceptualised an interactive visual tool with which the users can explore the anatomy and customise a given character set with its visual characteristics.
